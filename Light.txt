What is the path of light called? | Rectilinear propagation (Light travels in a straight line)
Which object can change the direction of light falling on it? | A Mirror
The phenomenon of bouncing back of light by a surface is called? | Reflection
What kind of image is formed by a plane mirror? | Virtual, erect, and of the same size
In a plane mirror, the left side of the object appears as the right side. This is called? | Lateral Inversion
Which mirror has a reflecting surface that curves inward? | Concave mirror
Which mirror has a reflecting surface that curves outward? | Convex mirror
Which mirror is used by dentists to see an enlarged image of teeth? | Concave mirror
Which mirror is used as a side-view mirror in vehicles to see a wider area? | Convex mirror
An image that can be obtained on a screen is called a? | Real image
An image that cannot be obtained on a screen is called a? | Virtual image
Which lens is thicker in the middle than at the edges? | Convex lens (Magnifying glass)
Which lens is thinner in the middle than at the edges? | Concave lens
Which lens is also known as a converging lens? | Convex lens
Which lens is also known as a diverging lens? | Concave lens
How many colors make up white light? | Seven
The band of seven colors produced when light passes through a prism is called? | Spectrum
Which natural phenomenon shows the dispersion of sunlight? | Rainbow
