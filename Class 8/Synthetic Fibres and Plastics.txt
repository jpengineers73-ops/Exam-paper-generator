What are synthetic fibres? | Man-made fibres prepared from chemicals
Name some synthetic fibres. | Nylon, Polyester, Acrylic, Rayon
What is rayon? | Artificial silk made from wood pulp
What is nylon? | Strong synthetic fibre used in ropes and fabrics
What is polyester? | Synthetic fibre used in clothing
What is acrylic? | Synthetic fibre used as wool substitute
Why are synthetic fibres popular? | They are strong, durable, and lightweight
What are plastics? | Polymers that can be molded into different shapes
What is polymer? | Substance made of many repeating units
What is polythene? | A common plastic used for bags
What are thermoplastics? | Plastics that can be melted and reshaped
Give examples of thermoplastics. | Polythene, PVC
What are thermosetting plastics? | Plastics that cannot be remelted
Give examples of thermosetting plastics. | Bakelite, Melamine
Why is Bakelite used in electrical switches? | It is a poor conductor of heat and electricity
Why is melamine used for utensils? | It is heat resistant
Why are plastics non-reactive? | They do not react easily with chemicals
Why are plastics useful? | Lightweight, durable, and corrosion-resistant
Why are plastics harmful to environment? | They are non-biodegradable
What is biodegradable material? | Material that can be decomposed by microorganisms
What is non-biodegradable material? | Material that does not decompose easily
Why should we avoid plastic bags? | They cause pollution
What is recycling? | Reusing plastic materials
What is the full form of PVC? | Polyvinyl Chloride
What are the 3 R’s? | Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
