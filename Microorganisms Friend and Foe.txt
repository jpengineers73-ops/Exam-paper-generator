What are microorganisms? | Very small organisms that cannot be seen with naked eyes
Name four types of microorganisms. | Bacteria, Fungi, Protozoa, Algae
Which instrument is used to see microorganisms? | Microscope
Name a useful bacterium. | Lactobacillus
What is fermentation? | Conversion of sugar into alcohol by yeast
Name the microorganism used in making bread. | Yeast
Which gas is released during fermentation? | Carbon dioxide
What are pathogens? | Disease-causing microorganisms
Name a disease caused by bacteria. | Cholera
Name a disease caused by virus. | Common cold
Name a disease caused by protozoa. | Malaria
Name a disease caused by fungi. | Ringworm
How do microorganisms enter our body? | Through air, water, food, or contact
What are antibiotics? | Medicines that kill or stop growth of bacteria
Name a common antibiotic. | Penicillin
Who discovered penicillin? | Alexander Fleming
What are vaccines? | Substances that provide immunity against diseases
What is pasteurization? | Heating milk to kill harmful microorganisms
What is food poisoning? | Illness caused by contaminated food
What are preservatives? | Substances that prevent food spoilage
What is nitrogen fixation? | Conversion of atmospheric nitrogen into usable form
Name bacteria involved in nitrogen fixation. | Rhizobium
What is the role of microorganisms in soil? | Increase soil fertility
What is communicable disease? | Disease that spreads from one person to another
How can we prevent diseases caused by microorganisms? | By hygiene, vaccination, and clean food/water
