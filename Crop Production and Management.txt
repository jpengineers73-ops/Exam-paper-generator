What is crop? | Plants of the same kind grown on a large scale
What is agriculture? | Practice of growing crops and rearing animals
Name two Kharif crops. | Paddy, Maize
Name two Rabi crops. | Wheat, Gram
What is ploughing? | Loosening and turning of soil
Why is ploughing important? | It improves aeration and root growth
What is sowing? | Planting seeds in the soil
Which tool is used for sowing? | Seed drill
What is manure? | Organic substance from decomposed waste
What is fertilizer? | Chemical substance rich in nutrients
What is irrigation? | Supplying water to crops
Name traditional irrigation methods. | Moat, Chain pump, Dhekli, Rahat
Name modern irrigation methods. | Sprinkler, Drip system
What are weeds? | Unwanted plants growing with crops
What is weeding? | Removal of unwanted plants
Name a chemical used to kill weeds. | Weedicides (e.g., 2,4-D)
What is harvesting? | Cutting of mature crops
What is threshing? | Separating grains from stalks
What is winnowing? | Separating grain from chaff using wind
What is storage? | Keeping grains safe from pests and moisture
What is crop rotation? | Growing different crops alternately
Why is crop rotation important? | Maintains soil fertility
What are agricultural implements? | Tools used in farming (plough, hoe, cultivator)
What is animal husbandry? | Rearing animals for food and products
Why is proper storage important? | Prevents spoilage and loss
